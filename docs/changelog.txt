FastSMRW changelog
==================

0.1.7
-----

- Fixed: the post view now keeps a post's paragraph and line breaks instead of running everything onto one line.

0.1.6
-----

- Fixed: In global hotkeys mode, invisible keys could not be held down.
- Delete your own posts: press Delete on one of your posts (or use the new Delete button in the post view). In the invisible interface it's Alt+Windows+Delete, or Control+Windows+Delete in the Windows 8.1 keymap. It confirms first, which you can turn off in Settings under Confirmation.
- The post view now shows which app a post was made from ("via ...") when the server provides it.
- Removed an unused "Stop audio / speech" shortcut that duplicated Stop Media.
- Import a keymap: the Keyboard Manager has a new Import button that reads an old FastSM keymap (or a FastSMRW one a friend sent), converting the shortcuts to FastSMRW's actions and skipping any that already match a default.
- Fixed: saving a keymap now writes atomically, so an interrupted save can no longer blank the keymap file.
- A sound now plays when you open a new timeline (a thread, user, hashtag, list, search, and so on).
- New "Automatically load older posts when you reach the end" option in Settings under Timelines; when it's off, press Period (or Timeline menu, Load Older Posts) to load older posts on demand.
- Added behind-the-scenes logging for the invisible interface (global hotkeys and keyhook) to the log file, to help track down input issues.
- A direct message (or direct-visibility mention) arriving in Mentions or Notifications now plays the "messages" sound instead of the usual mentions chime.
- When a post you can see is edited, the change now shows up live over streaming (Mastodon); a post deleted at the source is now removed live too.
- A post that a filter hides — or that matched a server filter — no longer plays a new-post sound.


0.1.5
-----

- Each account can now have its own soundpack: open the new Account Settings dialog (Account menu, Ctrl+Shift+Comma, or the invisible interface) and pick one.
- New "Me" menu holding Lists, View Muted Users, View Blocked Users, View Follow Requests, and Followed Hashtags.
- Tidied the menus: New Post now lives in the Status menu and Refresh Timeline in the Timeline menu.
- The Status menu's Boost and Favorite now show a check mark when the focused post is already boosted or favorited.
- The Status menu shortcuts are consistent again (Reply R, Boost B, Favorite F, Quote Q).
- Shift+F1 now checks for updates.
- You can set separate confirmations for undoing an action (unboosting, unfavoriting, unblocking), independent of doing it.
- When you reply to a boosted post, the booster is offered as a recipient but left unchecked, so you can mention them only if you want to.
- New installs now auto-refresh every minute and have streaming on by default.
- Setting cached posts per timeline to 0 now turns caching off and clears the existing cache files.
- Fixed: a quoted post's link is no longer left sitting in the post's text, since the quote is already shown on its own.
- Real-time streaming (Mastodon) now covers Local, Federated, hashtag, and list timelines too, not just Home and Notifications.
- Sending a reply now plays the reply sound instead of the new-post sound.
- Fixed: a streamed mention no longer plays the notification chime when you've hidden mentions from the Notifications buffer.
- Streamed mentions now also drop into your Mentions timeline (Mastodon) when you have one open, not just Notifications.
- New master sound volume slider on the Audio settings tab.
- New Audio setting to turn off the chime played at the top or bottom of a timeline.
- Boost, favorite, and pin sounds now play only once the action actually succeeds, instead of the moment you press the key.
- Removed the "Reply indicator" speech option, which never did anything.


0.1.4
-----

- Follow or unfollow a post's author with Ctrl+L (also in the Status menu, L in the layer, and Alt+Windows+L in the invisible interface); it follows if you don't yet, and unfollows if you do.
- Open a user timeline, open a user profile, or follow now always show a menu with a "Type a handle…" option, so you can look up or follow anyone by handle even if they aren't in your timeline.


0.1.3
-----

- Close the current timeline with Ctrl+W (it used to be Backspace); Hide Window is now Ctrl+H.
- In the layer, W now closes the current timeline, Shift+H shows or hides the window, and H follows a hashtag.
- Follow a hashtag by pressing H on a post (Mastodon): a box opens pre-filled with that post's hashtags (blank if it has none), and you can type any tag to follow.
- New "Followed Hashtags" item in the Application menu (Mastodon) lists the hashtags you follow, where you can open a timeline for one or unfollow it.
- New invisible-interface actions: Follow a hashtag (Alt+Windows+H) and Manage followed hashtags (unbound by default).
- When you view a thread (Mastodon) and the post links to or quotes another fediverse post, that post's conversation is now folded into the thread you're reading.
- Bluesky now has a Notifications timeline (likes, reposts, follows, mentions, replies and quotes), and the "show mentions in notifications" setting works for it too.
- Bluesky gained a Mentions timeline (add it from New Timeline) that shows the posts that mention or reply to you.
- Fixed: quoted posts on Mastodon (the newer quote-post feature) now show up and are read out, instead of being silently dropped.
- Fixed: quoting a post (Quote / Q) now actually attaches the quoted post when you send it, on both Mastodon and Bluesky - before, it silently posted just your text.


0.1.2
-----

- Fixed: focusing a notification from certain servers (some non-Mastodon fediverse instances) could crash the app; it no longer does, and one bad post or notification can't take the app down.
- Profile dialogs now show the person's bio and follower, following and post counts even when you open the profile from a mention.
- Ctrl+A now selects all text in the profile and Post Info read-only text boxes so you can copy the whole thing.
- Fixed: a post could occasionally show twice in a timeline and trap the cursor so you couldn't move past it; duplicate posts are now prevented (and cleaned up from existing timelines on launch).


0.1.1
-----

- All timelines now cache to disk and reload instantly on restart - author timelines, threads, hashtags, searches, lists, remote feeds and people lists too, not just your standing feeds (they still re-check for new posts, and drop their cache when closed).
- Fixed: number settings such as the maximum posts per timeline no longer reset themselves - the field no longer shows a thousands separator (e.g. "20,000") that was invalidating the value.


0.1.0
-----

- Pin a timeline with Ctrl+P (Timeline menu) to lock it so it can't be closed; press again to unpin. Pinned tabs stay pinned across restarts.
- A user's pinned posts (Mastodon) now show at the top of their timeline, with newer posts always arriving below them.
- Pin or unpin your own post to your profile (Mastodon) by pressing P on it, in the window or the layer (added to the layer help).
- New assignable invisible-interface actions (Keyboard Manager, unbound by default): move the current timeline up or down, pin/unpin the timeline tab, and pin/unpin your post to your profile.
- Follow requests (Mastodon): open Application > View Follow Requests to see who asked to follow you and accept or reject them, or press Enter on a follow-request notification to accept or reject it.
- Polls (Mastodon): if a post has a poll you can still vote in, Post Info now shows the options with a Vote button (a checklist for multiple-choice polls); once you've voted or the poll has closed, it shows each option's votes and percentage with your own picks marked.
- New Behavior settings tab: choose what pressing Enter does on a post (view post, view thread, reply, or open links) and on a user (actions menu, view profile, or view their timeline); the default is still view post.
- The invisible interface's Alt+Win+Enter now does your Enter action (view post by default) instead of opening links, and the layer's Enter follows the same setting; "Open link in post" stays assignable but is unbound by default.
- New secondary interact on a post with Shift+Enter, set in the Behavior tab: Play media (default), View post, View thread, Reply, or Open links.
- Shift+Enter also works in the layer and as a new invisible-interface "Secondary action" key (Win+Alt+Shift+Enter by default, Ctrl+Win+Shift+Enter on the Windows 8.1 keymap).
- Play media (default Shift+Enter): audio streams in a small in-app player - Space plays/pauses, Left/Right arrows seek, Up/Down change volume, Escape stops and closes; video and images still open in your system player.
- When a post has more than one attachment, Play media asks which one to open.
- New Behavior option "Play audio in the background": audio plays with no player window; stop it with Ctrl+S (Application menu) or the new assignable "Stop media playback" invisible action (Ctrl+Win+Alt+S by default).
- Fixed: the invisible interface now loads more posts as you navigate toward the edge of a timeline or a gap, just like scrolling the window does, so it no longer gets stuck at a post with more available above or below it.
- Fixed: your own post could occasionally appear twice (most noticeable with reverse timelines); the duplicate is now prevented.
- Installer: FastSMRW now ships an installer alongside the portable zip, and an installed copy updates itself through the installer instead of the zip.
- Media is now announced with its type - "Image", "Video", "Audio" or "GIF", plus its alt text - both when reading a post and in Post Info.


0.0.8
-----

- Reorder your open timelines: press Shift+Up or Shift+Down while focused on the timelines list to move the current timeline earlier or later; the new order is remembered across restarts.
- Fixed: choosing a different keymap in the Keyboard Manager now switches to it right away, instead of only taking effect after a restart.


0.0.7
-----

- Reply, boost and favorite counts are now only spoken when they're above zero, so posts with no activity don't read "0 boosts, 0 favorites".
- Fixed: replying or opening a dialog while the window is hidden no longer throws you into the hidden window; focus returns to wherever you were.
- Fixed: dialogs opened from the invisible interface now reliably take focus even when the window was showing but not focused.
- New Timeline (Ctrl+T) now lets you open any of your Mastodon lists as a timeline.
- New Lists manager (Application > Lists): create, rename (with replies policy and "hide from Home"), delete, and open your Mastodon lists.
- The user profile dialog now has a Lists button to add the person to or remove them from your Mastodon lists.
- New Application menu items "View Muted Users" and "View Blocked Users" open your muted/blocked accounts as a people list (Mastodon).
- When replying (Mastodon), the people being replied to now appear as a check list beside the post box - uncheck anyone you don't want to mention instead of editing them out of the text.
- New Timelines setting "Reverse timelines (newest at the bottom)": show feeds oldest-first, with new posts arriving at the bottom (threads and searches keep their normal order).
- Compose now has a Media button (Mastodon) to attach images, video or audio to a post and give each one alt text; you can post media on its own with no text.


0.0.6
-----

- New Speech option: use absolute clock time (e.g. "3:45 PM") instead of relative ("5 minutes ago"); the date is added on the end only when the post isn't from today.
- Speech now lets you set the separator spoken between items, and add your own text before and after each individual item (e.g. wrap the handle in parentheses) in Configure Posts / Users / Notifications.
- Speech: each item can also be set to have no separator after it, so it runs straight into the next one.
- Fixed: link and account menus opened from a hidden window (invisible interface) now take focus properly.


0.0.5
-----

- Layer mode keys now match the main window: Space opens the thread, T opens a new timeline, O opens the post's links, and Enter shows post info.
- The layer's help (slash) now opens in its own window listing every key on its own line, closed with the Close button or Escape.
- Client filters (Timeline menu, or Ctrl+Shift+F): pick which kinds of posts a timeline shows - originals, replies, replies to you, threads, boosts, quotes, with/without media, your own posts - and hide anything not containing some text; hidden posts are just hidden, so new ones still arrive and stay filtered.
- Server filters (Application > Server Filters, Mastodon): create, edit and delete your account's keyword filters with their contexts, hide-or-warn action and expiry; posts your server marks as filtered are hidden, or read out with a "Filtered" note.
- Open a link from the focused post: press Ctrl+O (Status menu > Open Links) or the new Open Links button in Post info, and pick from a menu when the post has more than one - text links, the link preview, media, and the post itself.
- The invisible interface's post-URL shortcut now offers those same in-post links (picking from a menu when there's more than one) instead of always jumping straight to the post's web page.

- New Speech option "Repeat the item when you hit the top or bottom of a timeline": with the invisible interface or layer, choose whether bumping an edge re-speaks the current post (still plays the boundary sound either way).
- Portable mode: put a "userdata" folder next to FastSMRW.exe and all your settings, accounts and cache are stored there instead of in AppData, so you can carry FastSM on a USB stick.
- Fixed: adding your first account now loads it right away instead of showing nothing until you restart.
- Fixed: switching the Add Account type between Mastodon and Bluesky no longer leaves "bsky.social" filling in as your Mastodon instance.


0.0.4
-----

- Press Ctrl+W to hide the FastSMRW window (bring it back with the invisible interface's show/hide key).
- In Layer mode, press W to show or hide the window; the layer's help (slash) now lists it.
- New assignable "Open the layer" shortcut (Keyboard Manager): bind a key to call up the invisible layer from Hotkey or Keyhook mode, the same way Ctrl+Win+Space opens it in Layer mode (unbound by default).
- Mastodon: the compose character limit now matches your instance's real limit instead of always assuming 500.


0.0.3
-----

- Auto-update: Application > Check for Updates downloads and installs the newest build and restarts; a new Settings > Updates page lets you pick the Stable branch (version updates only) or Latest (every new build), and whether to check automatically at startup.

- Fixed: turning off "Show mentions in the Notifications timeline" now actually hides them (the setting was saved but not applied on startup).

- Remote timelines (Mastodon): New Timeline now offers "Remote Instance Timeline" (browse another server's local feed, e.g. mastodon.social) and "Remote User Timeline" (a user's posts from their home server, e.g. name@instance.social); replying, boosting and favoriting a remote post just work.

- Find in timeline: press Ctrl+F to search the current timeline, F3 for the next match and Shift+F3 for the previous one (also available as unbindable invisible-interface actions).

- New Settings > Speech options for how posts read: choose how content warnings are handled (show the warning only, the warning then the post, or ignore it), remove emoji from post text and/or display names, and collapse long leading @mention lists to keep the first few names.

- The Mentions timeline now shows each mention as the post itself (a regular post), not as an "X mentioned you" notification.

- Your reading position in each timeline is now remembered across restarts, so you come back to the post you were on instead of the top.
- Any timeline you open (an author's posts, a thread, a hashtag, a search, ...) now reopens after a restart, per account; closing a timeline forgets its saved position and cache.
- All your accounts now stay loaded, refresh at your auto-refresh interval, and stream in real time all at once (not just the one you're viewing), so switching accounts is instant and every account stays up to date.

- New Invisible interface (Settings > Invisible interface): global hotkeys drive FastSMRW from any window, speaking the post you land on and the timeline and place when you switch (e.g. "Home, 1 of 500").
- New Keyboard Manager (Application menu, or the Invisible interface tab): create, edit, unbind, and share custom keymaps; files are interchangeable with the desktop client.
- Ctrl+Win+W (invisible interface) shows or hides the FastSMRW window from anywhere, and the app remembers whether it was hidden across restarts.
- New Low-level keyhook mode (Invisible interface tab): captures shortcuts the Windows shell reserves - like Win+arrows - that global hotkeys can't grab.
- New Layer mode (Invisible interface tab): press an activation key (default Ctrl+Win+Space) to open a FastSM layer where bare arrows and letters drive the timeline from any window; press slash for a spoken list of the keys, Escape exits, and opening a dialog leaves the layer. Set the key with the "Change layer activation key" button.
- The Keyboard Manager now includes read-only built-in keymaps shipped with the app (drop .keymap files in the program's keymaps folder); Windows 10 and 8.1 keymaps are included.
- Fixed the Alt key sometimes staying stuck down after closing the app while holding Alt (e.g. Alt+F4) with the keyhook active.
- The window title bar no longer shows the last spoken message.
- Gaps left when you were away are now remembered and filled in automatically as you scroll down into them, so the timeline stays continuous.
- The scroll position for loading older posts is saved in the cache, so it keeps working after a restart or account switch.
- Timelines now load older posts automatically as you reach the bottom (seamless infinite scroll), in every timeline that supports it.
- Loading older posts keeps working after a restart even when your cache is smaller than what you'd scrolled through, including on Bluesky.
- Bluesky now loads up to 100 posts per request instead of 40, so timelines fill in faster.
- Single-letter shortcuts on a post (R, B, F, Q, E, U) no longer jump your place in the timeline.
- Bluesky now supports the same social features as Mastodon: threads, an author's posts, Followers / Following, and profile with follow / mute / block.
- Fixed the startup "ready" chime playing every time you switched accounts; it now plays only once at startup.
- The app now reopens to the account you were last using.
- Fixed being stuck at the cached number of posts after launching or switching accounts.
- Speech settings now cover posts, users, and notifications separately (Speech tab: Configure Posts / Users / Notifications).
- Notification rows now read who acted, what they did, the related post, and the time, and you can reorder those parts.
- People rows (Followers / Following / People search) now follow your Users speech settings.
- New setting (Timelines tab): "Show mentions in the Notifications timeline".
- The New Timeline dialog (Ctrl+T) can now open a Hashtag timeline, Search Posts, or Search People (Mastodon).
- Search People opens as a people list with the same multi-select and batch follow / mute / block as Followers and Following.


0.0.2
-----

- Open a post author's posts as a timeline: press U on a post, use the Status
  menu's Open User Timeline, or the View Author button in Post info.
- Open a user's profile with Ctrl+U (or the Status menu): their name, handle,
  bio, and follower / following / post counts, with buttons to view their posts,
  follow / unfollow, mute, block, hide or show their boosts, or open their
  profile in your browser. The relationship buttons reflect your current
  relationship and announce the result.
- New setting (Settings > Confirmation): ask before blocking a user. When on,
  blocking from a profile asks first.
- When a post involves more than one person (its author plus anyone it mentions,
  quotes, or boosts), U and Ctrl+U show a menu to choose whose posts or profile
  to open.
- Press Space on a post to open its thread (same as View Thread).
- View an account's Followers or Following list (buttons in the Ctrl+U profile):
  each opens as its own timeline of people.
- On a people list you can select one or more people (multi-select) and press
  Enter to follow, unfollow, mute, unmute, block, or unblock them all at once. U
  and Ctrl+U also work on a person in a list (open their posts or profile).
- Close the current timeline with Backspace, from anywhere in the window.
- Clear a timeline with Ctrl+Delete, or Clear All Timelines with Ctrl+Shift+Delete
  (also in the Timeline menu): empties the loaded posts and caches.
- Closing an opened timeline returns you to the timeline you opened it from,
  instead of whatever was next in the list.
