FastSMRW changelog
==================

0.0.1 (in development)
----------------------

Accounts
- Sign in to Mastodon (opens your browser to authorize) and Bluesky (handle and
  app password). The posting source shows as "FastSMRW".
- Multiple accounts; switch between them with Ctrl+[ and Ctrl+].
- Accounts are remembered between launches and stored securely on your PC.

Timelines
- Home timeline shows your cached posts instantly on launch, then refreshes.
- Refresh now loads every new post since you were last here, instead of only the
  newest page.
- Left/Right arrows move between timelines.
- Open more timelines with the New Timeline dialog (Ctrl+T): Local, Federated,
  Mentions, Bookmarks, and Favorites (Mastodon). Close an opened timeline with
  Delete. Go straight to a timeline with Ctrl+1-9.
- Each timeline remembers where you were: switching timelines or receiving new
  posts keeps you on the post you were reading instead of jumping to the top.
- Movement keys: Ctrl+Left/Right pick a movement unit (Same User, Thread, or a
  time gap of 1h/2h/6h/1d) and announce it; Ctrl+Up/Down then jump by that unit
  (e.g. to the next post by the same person, or the next post in the thread).
- Ctrl+Z (Go Back) returns you to where you were before a jump.
- Fixed switching between open timelines sometimes landing on an unselected row,
  so your place is now reliably restored (and announced) on every timeline.
- Fixed timelines re-downloading from scratch on every launch: they now load
  instantly from the cache and only fetch posts that are actually new.
- Notifications and mentions now always show newest first.
- Replying to (or composing against) a post no longer jumps your place back to
  the top of the timeline.

Reading and posting
- Single keys on a post: R reply, Q quote, F favorite, B boost, E edit your own
  post.
- Press Enter on a post for a Post info dialog: the full post (author, time,
  content warning, text, attachments, reply/boost/favorite counts) with quick
  action buttons (reply, boost, favorite, quote, open in browser).
- Open the selected post in your web browser (Status menu - Open in Browser).
- View a post's conversation thread (Status menu - View Thread, or the View
  Thread button in Post info): opens the whole thread - the posts it replies to
  and the replies below it - as its own timeline you can arrow through.
- Compose dialog for new posts, replies, quotes, and edits, with content warning,
  visibility, language, polls, and scheduling where the platform supports them.
- The remaining character count appears in the compose window's title bar.
- Replies pre-fill the @-mentions and keep the original post's content warning and
  visibility.
- Choose whether Enter sends a post or adds a new line (setting).

Sounds
- Earcons for actions and events (boost, favorite, sent, new posts, errors, list
  edges), using the original FastSM default soundpack, bundled with the app.
- Drop your own soundpack into the soundpacks folder to replace it.

Accessibility
- Built for screen readers: rich, configurable descriptions of posts (which
  details are read, and in what order) — the default order matches the Mac app.
- Screen-reader speech support via UniversalSpeech.

Settings
- A Settings dialog (Ctrl+,) organized into tabs:
  - General: send posts with Return or Ctrl+Return.
  - Timelines: how many posts to cache per timeline.
  - Audio: turn sounds on/off, pick a soundpack, open the soundpacks folder.
  - Speech: choose which post details are read, and reorder them.
  - Advanced: how many pages to load per refresh.
  - Confirmation: ask before boosting, favoriting, or clearing a timeline.
- Auto-refresh: optionally check your timelines for new posts every 30 seconds,
  minute, 2 minutes, or 5 minutes (Timelines tab). New posts play that
  timeline's sound, even for timelines you're not currently viewing.
- Real-time streaming (Mastodon): turn on "Stream in real time" (Timelines tab)
  to receive new home posts and notifications live as they happen.
- Settings are saved and applied immediately.
- Your accounts and preferences are now kept together in a single settings file
  (instead of two), and existing preferences carry over automatically.

Menus and window
- Application, Status, Timeline, and Account menus with familiar shortcuts.

Compatibility
- Runs on Windows 7 and later.
- This changelog is included with the app.
