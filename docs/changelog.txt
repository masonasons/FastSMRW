FastSMRW changelog
==================

0.0.1 (in development)
----------------------

Accounts
- Sign in to Mastodon (opens your browser to authorize) and Bluesky (handle and
  app password). The posting source shows as "FastSMRW".
- Multiple accounts; switch between them with Ctrl+[ and Ctrl+].
- Accounts are remembered between launches and stored securely on your PC.

Timelines
- Home timeline shows your cached posts instantly on launch, then refreshes.
- Refresh now loads every new post since you were last here, instead of only the
  newest page.
- Left/Right arrows move between timelines.
- Open more timelines (Local, Federated) with the New Timeline dialog (Ctrl+T);
  close an opened timeline with Delete. Go straight to a timeline with Ctrl+1-9.
- Each timeline remembers where you were: switching timelines or receiving new
  posts keeps you on the post you were reading instead of jumping to the top.

Reading and posting
- Single keys on a post: R reply, Q quote, F favorite, B boost, E edit your own
  post, Ctrl+I for full post info.
- Compose dialog for new posts, replies, quotes, and edits, with content warning,
  visibility, language, polls, and scheduling where the platform supports them.
- The remaining character count appears in the compose window's title bar.
- Replies pre-fill the @-mentions and keep the original post's content warning and
  visibility.
- Choose whether Enter sends a post or adds a new line (setting).

Sounds
- Earcons for actions and events (boost, favorite, sent, new posts, errors, list
  edges), using the original FastSM default soundpack, bundled with the app.
- Drop your own soundpack into the soundpacks folder to replace it.

Accessibility
- Built for screen readers: rich, configurable spoken descriptions of posts
  (which details are read, and in what order, is configurable).
- Screen-reader speech support via UniversalSpeech.

Menus and window
- Application, Status, Timeline, and Account menus with familiar shortcuts.

Compatibility
- Runs on Windows 7 and later.
