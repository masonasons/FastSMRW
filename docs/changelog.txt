FastSMRW changelog
==================

0.0.3 (in development)
----------------------

- New: an Invisible interface. Settings has a new "Invisible interface" tab where
  you can turn on Global hotkeys to drive FastSMRW from any window - move through
  your timeline, reply, boost, favorite, switch timelines and accounts, and more,
  without switching to the app. Navigation speaks the post you land on, and
  switching timelines announces the timeline and your place in it (e.g. "Home,
  1 of 500"). (Still to
  come: a low-level keyhook mode that can also grab key combos Windows reserves
  for itself - like Win+arrows - plus a full keyboard manager to customize the
  shortcuts.)

- When a refresh brings in new posts that don't connect to what you already had
  (you were away a while), the app now remembers the gap and fills it in
  automatically as you scroll down into it - so the timeline stays continuous
  instead of silently jumping. These gaps are saved with the cache too.

- The scroll position for loading older posts is now saved in the cache, so
  scrolling down to load more keeps working after you restart the app or switch
  accounts (previously it only worked in the session where you first loaded the
  timeline). Note: this needs the cache to hold the posts you'd loaded - if you
  set a high "API calls per timeline load" but a low "Maximum posts to cache",
  raise the cache so the two match.

- Timelines now load older posts automatically as you reach the bottom (seamless
  infinite scroll), in every timeline that supports it - this previously did not
  happen at all, so you were stuck at the first batch. Each load pulls as many
  pages as your "API calls per timeline load" setting (Advanced).

- Loading older posts now keeps working after a restart even when your "Maximum
  posts to cache" is smaller than what you'd scrolled through - including on
  Bluesky. The cache is trimmed to a real page boundary so it can pick up exactly
  where it left off.

- Bluesky now loads up to 100 posts per request instead of 40 (its API's limit),
  so timelines fill in faster with fewer round-trips.

- Single-letter shortcuts on a post (R reply, B boost, F favorite, Q quote,
  E edit, U author) no longer jump your place in the timeline. The posts list
  was quietly treating those keys as a type-to-search and moving the selection,
  which is why replying and then escaping flung you to the top. Your position
  now stays put.

- Bluesky now supports the same social features as Mastodon: view a post's
  thread, open an author's posts, open Followers / Following lists, and the
  profile with follow / unfollow, mute, and block.
- Fixed the startup "ready" chime playing every time you switched accounts - it
  now plays only once when the app finishes starting.
- The app now reopens to the account you were last using.
- Fixed being stuck at the cached number of posts after launching or switching
  accounts: loading older posts (scrolling down) works again.

- Speech settings now cover three kinds of rows separately. The Speech tab has
  Configure Posts, Configure Users, and Configure Notifications buttons; each
  opens a dialog to check which details are spoken and reorder them (Ctrl+Up /
  Ctrl+Down or the Move buttons).
- Notification rows now read who acted, what they did, the related post, and the
  time - and you can choose/reorder those parts (Speech > Notifications).
- People rows (Followers / Following / People search) now follow your Users
  speech settings instead of always reading just name and handle.
- New setting (Timelines tab): "Show mentions in the Notifications timeline" (on
  by default). Turn it off to hide mentions there - handy when you keep a
  separate Mentions timeline open.

- The New Timeline dialog (Ctrl+T) can now open a Hashtag timeline, Search Posts,
  or Search People (Mastodon) - pick the type and type the hashtag or search text.
- Search People opens as a people list with the same multi-select and batch
  follow / mute / block as Followers and Following.


0.0.2
-----

- Open a post author's posts as a timeline: press U on a post, use the Status
  menu's Open User Timeline, or the View Author button in Post info.
- Open a user's profile with Ctrl+U (or the Status menu): their name, handle,
  bio, and follower / following / post counts, with buttons to view their posts,
  follow / unfollow, mute, block, hide or show their boosts, or open their
  profile in your browser. The relationship buttons reflect your current
  relationship and announce the result.
- New setting (Settings > Confirmation): ask before blocking a user. When on,
  blocking from a profile asks first.
- When a post involves more than one person (its author plus anyone it mentions,
  quotes, or boosts), U and Ctrl+U show a menu to choose whose posts or profile
  to open.
- Press Space on a post to open its thread (same as View Thread).
- View an account's Followers or Following list (buttons in the Ctrl+U profile):
  each opens as its own timeline of people.
- On a people list you can select one or more people (multi-select) and press
  Enter to follow, unfollow, mute, unmute, block, or unblock them all at once. U
  and Ctrl+U also work on a person in a list (open their posts or profile).
- Close the current timeline with Backspace, from anywhere in the window.
- Clear a timeline with Ctrl+Delete, or Clear All Timelines with Ctrl+Shift+Delete
  (also in the Timeline menu): empties the loaded posts and caches.
- Closing an opened timeline returns you to the timeline you opened it from,
  instead of whatever was next in the list.
