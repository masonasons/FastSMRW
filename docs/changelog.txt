FastSMRW changelog
==================

0.0.2 (in development)
----------------------

- Open a post author's posts as a timeline: press U on a post, use the Status
  menu's Open User Timeline, or the View Author button in Post info.
- Open a user's profile with Ctrl+U (or the Status menu): their name, handle,
  bio, and follower / following / post counts, with buttons to view their posts
  or open their profile in your browser.
- Press Space on a post to open its thread (same as View Thread).
